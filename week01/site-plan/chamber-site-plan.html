About us

The creation of the Argentine Chamber of Culture, Commerce, Industry and Production, on November 7, 1924, which became the Argentine Chamber of Commerce from 1926, took place at a time when the world began to move towards a new paradigm. , which would generate enormous economic, political and social consequences for Argentina.

Until then, a succession of circumstances that occurred from the second half of the last century had given impetus to the development in our country of a model of primary export economy, which came to place us among the ten most prosperous nations of the time

Site Purpose

“The Argentina Chamber of Commerce acts as a regional economic catalyst, leadership convener and community champion.”

To be the leading business union in the country, promoter of formal trade, economic growth and institutional strengthening.

The Chamber of Commerce, Industry and Production of Resistencia is the union entity that brings together the commerce, industry and production of goods and services sector. Its main mission is union representation, accompaniment and management in the face of problems that afflict members. In addition, it provides specialized advice with professionals from different areas; constant training cycles; sales promotional actions; and commercial reports among other services and benefits.
